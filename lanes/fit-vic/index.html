How a FlightLine Fitness Session Works

Every session follows the same simple flight plan:

  1. Scan In — You book and load the DSX Pack that matches your mission.
  2. Briefing — Vic reads your goals, current state, and constraints.
  3. Execution — Kinetic work. Clear sets, reps, timing, and intensity.
  4. Proof — Outcome, notes, and upgrades flow into the Motion Ledger.
  5. Next Route — If needed, you’re routed to nutrition, recovery, or another lane.

Choose Your Mission

  • Baseline Flight Check — test, measure, and map where you are now.
  • Build Cycle — multi-week strength or conditioning focus.
  • Cut / Recomp — tighten up while staying functional.
  • Game-Time Ready — short runway, clear deadline, maximum focus.
